How DeepSentry Works
4-step AI analysis pipeline
1
Facial Geometry Mapping
Analyzes 3D facial structure, landmark positions and symmetry patterns to detect manipulation.
2
Temporal Consistency Check
For videos, checks frame-to-frame coherence and detects unnatural blending or warping artifacts.
3
Generative Artifact Scan
Detects GAN/diffusion model fingerprints — pixel-level noise patterns unique to AI-generated content.
4
Deep Learning Inference
Vision Transformer (ViT) model runs final classification with a confidence score from 0–100%.
